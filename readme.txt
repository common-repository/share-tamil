== Description ==

I am TechTamil Karthik.  I am happy to release this wordpress plugin for Tamil wordpress bloggers.

Tamil blog aggregator websites are very popular like Tamilmanam, Tamil 10, Indli. 
WP Share Tamil plugin helps you and your users to share blog posts directly from your blog to social networks & tamil sites.
like Tamilmaman, Tamil 10, Indli, facebook, twitter, linkedin etc.
This version of WP Share Tamil plugin supports following social networks & Tamil sites

Want to see LIVE DEMO?  http://www.techtamil.com/computer-tips-tricks-in-tamil/wordpress-plugin-share-tamil/

* Tamil 10

* Twitter	
* Facebook	
* Google Plus	
* Linkedin	
* Stumbleupon	
* Digg	
* reddit	
* Evernote

You can
1. Enable/disable any of the above social share buttons from admin panel


== Installation ==

= Step 1 =

1. Download zip file and Extract it 
2. Upload wp-share-tamil folder to your /wp-content/plugins/ folder.
2. Go to the 'Plugins' page in the menu and activate the plugin.

= Step 2 =

1. Go to Wodpress Admin Panel > Setting > Tamil Share
2. Enable/Disable or select Tamil Sites & Social Share Buttons and Update it. 

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Can I disable Social Share Buttons from Post? =

Yes, Go to Wodpress Admin Panel > Setting > Share Tamil, From Posts Setting uncheck the enable box and update it.
